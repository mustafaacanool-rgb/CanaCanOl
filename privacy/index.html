CanaCanOl - Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the CanaCanOl mobile application (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects user information. By using the app, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Currently, the application only uses Firebase services provided by Google. Firebase may collect anonymous usage data such as device model, operating system version, crash reports, and general app interaction statistics. This information does not personally identify users and is used solely to improve app performance and stability.

2. How We Use the Information

The collected data may be used for the following purposes:

  • To monitor and improve app performance,
  • To fix bugs and crashes,
  • To enhance user experience and develop new features,
  • To analyze general app usage trends.

3. Third-Party Services

At this time, the app only integrates with Google Firebase services. However, in the future, we may use additional services such as analytics tools, advertising networks, or push notification providers. If any new third-party service is added, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

For more information about Firebase’s data practices, please visit: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4. Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored securely on Firebase’s infrastructure. CanaCanOl does not sell, rent, or share personal information with any third party. We take reasonable measures to ensure data security and protect against unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with information, we will delete it from our servers as soon as possible.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the “Last Updated” date will be revised accordingly. We encourage users to check this page periodically for updates.
